What is Ethereum?

Ethereum is more than just a cryptocurrency - it's a decentralized computing platform that runs smart contracts: applications that run exactly as programmed without any possibility of downtime, censorship, fraud, or third-party interference.

Smart Contracts

Self-executing contracts with the terms directly written into code.

DeFi

Decentralized finance applications revolutionizing traditional banking.

NFTs

Digital assets proving ownership of unique items or content.

Why Ethereum Matters

  • Enables true digital ownership and asset transfer
  • Creates new possibilities for financial inclusion
  • Powers the next generation of internet applications
